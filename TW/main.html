Tribal Wars Stats Viewer - Help Guide
üéØ Basic Features
  • Server/World Selection: Use the dropdowns at the top to select which Tribal Wars server and world you want to view statistics for.
  • Metric Selection: Choose which statistic to track (Points, Rank, Villages, OD, ODA, ODD, Daily stats, etc.).
  • Search: Type in the search box to find players or tribes. Click on a result to add them to your chart.
üìä Chart Legend
  • Player Rankings: Each player's name in the legend shows their current global rank for the selected metric in brackets. For example, "PlayerName (#5)" means they're ranked 5th globally for that metric.
  • Click Legend Items: Click on any name in the legend to show/hide that player or tribe on the chart.
  • Zoom: Click and drag on the chart to zoom in. Double-click to reset zoom.
üèÜ Top 10 Players
  • Once a world is loaded, you'll see buttons for "Top 10 [Metric]" below the search box.
  • Click any button to add the top 10 players for that specific metric to your chart.
  • This is great for comparing yourself to the leaders!
üë• Tribe Grouping
  • Group Tribe: When you have tribes selected, check this option to combine all tribe members into a single line showing tribe totals.
  • Use Average: When grouping tribes, check this to show the average per player instead of the total.
üîó Sharing & URLs
  • The URL automatically updates as you make selections.
  • You can share your URL with others, and they'll see the same chart!
  • Bookmark URLs to quickly return to your favorite views.
  • URL includes: server, world, metric, selected players/tribes, and top 10 groups.
